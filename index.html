Our company

Our company started in 2003, a 100% owned Filipino entrepreneurship. Being a Chemical Engineer, passing the achieved global standardization in quality hair, our company is the first to test and introduce Philippine virgin hair to the global market, joining the ranks of European, Indian, Chinese, and other Asian hair. It's our goal to help other people with their hair loss due to cancer and other ailments, and that it is our moral obligation to give them hope in our own little way.

TEXTURES

Our company does Textures such as 'Silky Straight','Slightly Coarse', 'Coarse' or 'Natural Wavy'.

WAVY

We also labor and put effort into Wavy and curls, which includes, 'Deep Wavy', 'Loose Wavy' and 'C-Curl'.

CURLS

Our supplied Filipino hair also includes other different types of Curly hair, this includes hair such as 'Mulatto Curl' or "Curly'.
